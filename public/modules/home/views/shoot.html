About Us

OFFUZ is a new start-up established in 2015 by a small team of digital experts with experience across the loyalty and offers space, as well as ecom/mcom and payments.

They know how the big corporates use these attributes to generate consumer excitement and engagement in order to increase sales and revenue. But in their experience, the smaller businesses are left behind or given a minimal service. We recognise the importance of small and medium businesses to global economy, and their needs have so far been unmet in a truly collaborative and inclusive solution. That is why OFFUZ was established.
